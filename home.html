About us

We are a group of four students from the IUT Claude Bernard (Lyons, France), currently studying Informatics for two years.

We're not mental health issues' specialists but we still want to help by creating a community where people can share their experience and learn more about this sensitive field. There are so much biases about mental health diseases that we want to break.
